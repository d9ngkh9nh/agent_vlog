About Me

The person behind
the wardrobe.

Hi, I'm Alex — a fashion writer, slow fashion advocate, and chronic thrift-store addict based in Hanoi. I started Clozet in 2019 as a personal archive and it somehow grew into something much bigger.

I believe getting dressed should feel intentional, not anxious. Everything I write here is in pursuit of that feeling.
